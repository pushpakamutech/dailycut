a story about food.

The Daily Cut is based on the simple premise that it shouldn’t have to be
a struggle to eat clean. With the right ingredients and preparation techniques, anyone
will be able to not only embark on a clean eating journey, but enjoy it as well.

what exactly goes into your food ?

The Daily Cut adheres to two basic principles across its entire menu - whole ingredients and freshly-prepared. This is why you will never find any processed foods in our open kitchens or service counters, where everything is made before your eyes.
